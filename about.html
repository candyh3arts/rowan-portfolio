I'm Rowan

An artist and web developer with a lifelong love of color, clean structures, and storytelling. My background in fine arts and craft (drawing, painting, textile art, and illustration) taught me to see design as both structure and expression. As a front-end web developer I focus on creating visually unified experiences.

I care deeply about cohesion and making every element feel like it belongs. Whether I'm refining a layout grid or picking the perfect palette, I aim for a balance of warmth with precision. I love projects that combine technical organization with creative personality!

How I Got Here

Where it began

Drawing has always been part of my life. When I discovered website designers in the early 2010s, I suddenly had a place to combine art, color, and layout. It was the first time I realized how much I loved shaping how things look and feel.

Discovering design

In high school, I attended SCAD's Rising Star program for UX Design. It was my first deep dive into visual hierarchy, color, and usability. Seeing how design could be both expressive and practical made everything click for me.

Building for the web

As a web development student at SNHU, I've shifted from UX into hands-on development. Every project helps me learn more, and I love merging clean code with thoughtful, expressive design.
